Project 01

Building Emissions Benchmark & Decarbonisation Tool

A multi-step web application that takes a building owner through energy input collection, country-specific grid emission factors, Scope 1 & 2 calculation, EU peer benchmarking, CSRD readiness scoring, and a projected net-zero pathway to 2050.

Covers 20 European countries, six building types, and produces a full decarbonisation trajectory — mirroring the reporting and optimisation logic at the core of comundo's product.

Project 02

CSRD Readiness Assessment Framework

A comprehensive 10-section ESRS assessment tool covering all Environmental (E1–E5), Social (S1–S4), and Governance (G1) disclosure areas. Guides companies through 40+ weighted questions to produce a readiness score, gap analysis, prioritised action plan, and CSRD compliance timeline.

Directly relevant to comundo's core value proposition: helping building owners get their data in order before their CSRD deadline.

Project 03

CO₂ Benchmark Calculator

A lightweight React component for instant commercial real estate carbon footprint estimation. Selectable building type, floor area, energy source, building age, and occupancy rate. Outputs carbon intensity, total emissions, Scope 1/2/3 breakdown, performance rating, and benchmark comparison against EU Taxonomy, Danish Energy Class A, BREEAM Excellent, and the EPBD regulatory minimum — based on the Vesterbro Retail Partners A/S case study.

About me

I'm Joatham Jacob, a sustainability professional with an MSc in Corporate Sustainability from Università degli Studi di Milano, Italy. My academic work focused on GHG accounting, corporate sustainability strategy, and the EU regulatory landscape — including CSRD, the EU Taxonomy, and EPBD.

Through several internships in sustainability and decarbonization, I've developed practical experience translating climate frameworks into tools that clients can actually use. I'm drawn to the intersection of sustainability expertise and software — the space comundo occupies.

I built these three tools independently to demonstrate that I can contribute from day one: understanding what a property owner in Copenhagen needs to know about their Scope 2 emissions, what a CSRD gap looks like in practice, and how to communicate decarbonisation pathways in a way that drives real decisions.

I'm early in my career and I'm looking to learn from the best while adding genuine value. comundo's mission — making ESG data as integrated as financial data — is exactly the kind of challenge I want to work on.
